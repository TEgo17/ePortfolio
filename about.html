About Me

Here's something about me...

BIO

I am an undergraduate student at the Univeristy of Toronto Mississauga. I am currently in my third year of studies; I am majoring statistics while minoring in both mathematics and computer science. I am particulary interested in machine learning and software development. Some of my hobbies outside of school consist of playing sports, creating music and exploring new things!

Sports

My favourite sport growing up was baseball. I have been playing organized baseball for about 8 years now. The aspect I love the most about the sport of baseball is the fact that it is team oritented. Everyone must play at their best for the team to succeed; from the pitcher to the outfielders to the catcher. Additionally, I love playing basketball recreationally. Over the last couple of years it has slowly been one of my favourite pastimes.

Music

One of the best ways to pass time is to listen to music. There are definately many different genres of music out there, but my favourite genres would have to be rap, hip-hop and occasionally classic (depending on my mood). I also like to create music in my free time. I have previosuly created beats and melodies using FLStudio. One really important takeaway I learned from creating music is having patience is key as the best things come to those who wait.

Exploring new things

Trying new things is always important in life in any aspect. Whether it is a new food, a new sport or doing something you would not do on a regular basis; it is vital when it comes to character development. Personally, I was a very quiet kid growing up and I despised talking and meeting new people. However, univeristy has taught me critical skills in communication and networking. It is essential to meet new people to share new experiences, new knowledge and new memories.
